I am mostly interested in interacting electron-phonon-systems.

The "end goal" of my research is to have a single "button" (metaphorically; it will be a script that interfaces many different first principles codes and post-processing tools) that computes for an arbitrary material the resistivity as a function of temperature (and pressure). In particular: It computes at which temperature a material can become superconducting (by determining whether non-trivial solutions to Eliashberg-like-equations exist) and whether this state is energetically favorable (by comparing the grand canonical potential of the superconducting state with that of the normal state).
